## pre_clean.csv and post_clean.csv
Gender: 1 = Man, 2 = Woman
Race: 1 = American Indian/Alaska Native, 2 = Asian, 3 = Native Hawaiin/Pacific Islander, 4 = Black/African 
American, 5 = White, 6 = A race not listed
Ethnicity: 1 = Hispanic or Latino, 2 = Not Hispanic or Latino 
All questions are on a 0-10 scale, except for FirstMeet and Interact, which are on a 1-6 scale 

## video_data.csv
Time: In seconds from start of video
SubjAssign: 1 means a subject was assigned as the primary contributor for this step, 0 means this step does not 
require a contributor
Videos coded using BORIS video coding software

## transcript_info.csv
num_utterances: Number of times participant spoke
positivity: semantic analysis from 0 (negative) to 1 (positive)
first: number of first-person pronouns uttered
